About KM Mayawati Government Girls Polytechnic

Established in 2002, KM Mayawati Government Girls Polytechnic, Badalpur is a leading institute dedicated to providing quality technical education exclusively for women. Affiliated with the Uttar Pradesh Board of Technical Education (UPBTE) and approved by AICTE, New Delhi, the college aims to empower young women with knowledge, skills, and confidence to build successful careers. Spread across 7 acres, the campus is equipped with modern classrooms, computer labs, workshops, library, hostel, and sports facilities. We currently offer diploma programs in Computer Science, Electronics Engineering, and Information Technology. Our dedicated faculty and staff work with a vision to create professionals who are not only technically strong but also responsible citizens. KM Mayawati Government Girls Polytechnic continues to achieve recognition as one of the reputed polytechnic institutions in Uttar Pradesh.

Achievements & Recognition

Accreditation: The polytechnic is recognized by AICTE, ensuring that the courses offered meet national standards of quality education.

Student Strength: Currently, the institution enrolls approximately 340 students across its various diploma programs.

Faculty Expertise: The polytechnic boasts a dedicated team of 15 faculty members who are committed to providing high-quality education and mentorship to students.

Infrastructure: The campus is equipped with modern infrastructure, including well-equipped laboratories, a computer center, library, hostel accommodations, and sports facilities.

Vision and Mission

The vision of KM Mayawati Government Girls Polytechnic is to be a center of excellence in technical education for women, fostering innovation, skill development, and leadership. The mission is to provide quality education that empowers women to become self-reliant and contribute effectively to society and the economy.
